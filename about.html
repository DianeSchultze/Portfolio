Hey! I'm Diane Schultze, a designer and developer who loves creating digital work that feels both functional and full of personality. I enjoy building interfaces that are clean, engaging, and easy for people to use.

My work usually lives at the intersection of front-end development and visual design. I like turning ideas into polished web experiences, whether that means coding responsive layouts, planning content, or shaping the overall look and feel of a project.

I’m especially drawn to projects that let me mix thoughtful UX, playful aesthetics, and strong technical structure. That balance is a big part of how I approach my portfolio and case studies too.
